Animator, Rigger, Designer

Welcome! If you need help with modeling, rigging, texturing, lighting, rendering, animation, storyboarding and environmental designs, look no further! I can make your designs come to life! I am knowledgeable in Autodesk Maya, Adobe Photoshop, Storyboard Pro just to name a few. Feel free to browse my sample work.

About me: I graduated from Eastern Michigan University with a Bachelor in Science in Simulation, Animation and Gaming, with Cum Laude honors. I believe in hard work and self sufficiency having worked as life guard starting in high school and all throughout my college years in the University's Recreation Center.

I live in a small rural town in Michigan where I enjoy fishing, hunting, hiking, and biking. On my spare time, I love playing D&D and Warhammer games with my friends. I also find painting my miniature Warhammer pieces fun and a source of creative inspiration.
